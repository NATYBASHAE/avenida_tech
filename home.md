narrative flow.

A premium homepage is psychological sequencing.

Every scroll section should answer a question in the visitor’s mind before they consciously ask it.

Like this:

Who are these people?
Are they professional?
What exactly do they do?
Can they handle serious projects?
Do they understand my industry?
Can I trust them technically?
Have they done this before?
How do I contact them?

That is homepage architecture.

Most business websites fail because they:

dump information randomly
use generic sections
have no visual hierarchy
no emotional progression
no perceived authority building

Your homepage must feel:

cinematic
engineered
expensive
operational
technically competent
